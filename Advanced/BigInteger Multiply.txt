Given two BigIntegers X and Y. You have to multiply X and Y.

 

Example 1:

Input:
X = 3, Y = 4
Output:
12
Explanation:
Multiplication of X and Y is 12
 

Example 2:

Input:
X = 8, Y = 2 
Output:
16
Explanation:
Multiplication of X and Y is 16.

Solution:-
class MathematicalOperation{
    
    static BigInteger mul(BigInteger x, BigInteger y){
    
       x=x.multiply(y);
       return x;
    }
    
}
